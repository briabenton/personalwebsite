about me

Bria Gabrielle Benton.

Not to be confused with any other Bria, because let's face it, there are a few of them running around south Atlanta.

movie fanatic.

suspense thrillers. action. dramas. I have a giant movie collection and I'm not afraid to quote from any movie. IMDB and Roger Ebert are the homies.

hopeless romantic.

I cry while watching Wedding Planner. I root for Julia Roberts in My Best Friend's Wedding. I swoon for flowers and chocolates.

drama queen.

I've always been the girl to cry twice as hard, smile twice as large, and yell twice as loud. If eveyone else is laughing, I'm slapping my knees, rolling on the floor in stitches.

traveler.

I plan to travel to every continent by the age of 30. So far I've made it to Paris, London, Montreal, and several cities in the States. Trying to fill my passport.
